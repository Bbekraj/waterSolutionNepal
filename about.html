Our Journey

From Startup to Nepal's #1 Water Company

Built on engineering excellence, client trust, and an unwavering commitment to clean water for every Nepali business.

"We didn't just want to sell water filters — we wanted to solve Nepal's water problems with engineering, science, and service that lasts a lifetime."
👤
Ram Prasad Shrestha
Founder & CEO
2015
Foundation & First Projects

Water Solution Nepal Pvt. Ltd. was registered in Kathmandu. Our first three RO installations — a school, a clinic, and a small hotel — set the standard we still uphold today.

2017
Industrial Expansion

Secured our first large industrial contract — a 10,000 LPH plant for a Terai-based beverage company. Expanded team to 20 engineers and launched our in-house water testing lab.

2019
ISO 9001 Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management certification — the first water treatment company in Nepal to do so. Expanded service coverage to all 7 provinces.

2021
Hospital & Healthcare Division

Launched a dedicated healthcare water division, delivering dialysis-grade and pharmaceutical purified water systems to 12 major hospitals across Nepal.

2024
200+ Projects Milestone

Surpassed 200 completed installations. Launched IoT-enabled smart monitoring systems and solar-powered RO plants for remote communities. Team now 50+ strong.
